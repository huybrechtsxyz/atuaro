## Welcome
Hi everyone and welcome to the session zero of your Atuaro adventure.

Just a reminder that in the Player Handbook you can find a lot of interesting things like:
* [Basic Play](../../player_handbook/02_basic_play.md)
* [Table Rules](../../player_handbook/03_table_rules.md)
* [Game Rules](../../player_handbook/10_game_rules.md)

As mentioned in the Table Rules, I expect that you have a way of managing your character. The obvious ways is using a form fillable PDF or one of the many D&D character apps that are available. A few examples are the [Standard Character Sheet](../../player_handbook/assets/character_sheets/character_sheet_standard.pdf) or an [Alternative Character Sheet](../../player_handbook/assets/character_sheets/character_sheet_alternative.pdf). My personal preference is that you no longer use D&D Beyond in light of the O.G.L. controversy. An editable PDF, World Anvil, VTT has a good player character manager my preference would be one of these.
